🔑 Key Strategic Signals

Pezeshkian's apology to Gulf states is Iran's clearest diplomatic signal yet — a tacit acknowledgment that attacking Gulf neighbors is counterproductive and risking broader isolation.

Russia's real-time satellite intelligence to Iran is an escalatory act that functionally makes Russia a co-belligerent. This raises Scenario C probability.

Iran's oil infrastructure now targeted — Shahran, Aghdasieh, and Tehran refinery strikes mark a strategic escalation aimed at fuel starvation of Iranian forces.

China-Iran Hormuz corridor negotiations remain the most important wildcard. A China-brokered passage deal could partially reopen shipping and cap oil prices.
